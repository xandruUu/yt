# Copyright And Safety

ShortsFactory treats copyright and monetization safety as workflow gates, not afterthoughts.

## Required records

- Every asset needs source and license data.
- Every external clip/image/audio import needs source, license notes, and commercial-use confirmation.
- Every music track needs source, license, attribution, and monetization safety data.
- Export packages include `license_manifest.json`.

## Not allowed in MVP

- Downloading social-media clips.
- Scraping protected content.
- Using music without a license record.
- Using third-party visuals without a license record.
- Rendering or exporting imported external assets before human license review.
- Automatic YouTube upload or publication.
- Fake facts, deepfakes, impersonation, or evasive copyright behavior.
